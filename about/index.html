We are LARRY, FIRST® (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) Robotics Team 4606, a Community team based in Calgary AB, Sponsored by APEGA, Qsine among others. Robotics team. LARRY is a student led team established in late 2012 to participate in the first annual Western Regional FIRST® Robotics Competition 2013. This year’s challenge was called Ultimate Ascent, and we are proud to say that as a small rookie team, Placing in the 5th place Alliance amongst a 30 team field.
